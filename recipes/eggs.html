Sunny Side Up Eggs with Toast and Avocado

Cook up two delicious sunny side up eggs, and combine with toast and avocado for a healthy breakfast, lunch or dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • Prefered Bread
  • 1 Avocado
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Dried Thyme
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Place pan on burner and turn on to low heat.
  2. Once the pan is feeling warm, add a tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil and let it heat up for a minute.
  3. Carefully crack 2 large eggs into pan and add salt, freshly ground black pepper and a pinch of dried thyme. Cover with lid.
  4. Place two pieces of bread in toaster.
  5. Slice up a whole avocado while the bread is toasting. When the bread is finished, take out and butter.
  6. At this point, the eggs should be close to finished. Cook until the area around the yoke is no longer clear, and the yoke itself is starting to lightly fog over.
  7. Add eggs to place with eggs and avocado and enjoy!
