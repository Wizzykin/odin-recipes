Salmon and Asparagus

Description

Delicious baked salmon with a side of asparagus! Even your kids will love it!

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillets
  • Bunch of Asparagus [1]
  • Lemon [1]
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 400F with baking tray inside
  2. Place salmon fillets and place them on a cutting board
  3. Drizzle a small amount of EVOO on each fillet and coat evenly
  4. Add salt and pepper as desired
  5. Slice lemon into thin circles and place one or two on each fillet
  6. Cuts ends off asparagus and then toss in a bowl with a good coating of EVOO
  7. Add salt and pepper as desired
  8. When oven is heated, add Salmon and Asparagus directly onto the baking tray and bake for 15 mins
    • ***if you would like the salmon skin to not stick to the baking tray, add a thin layer of EVOO to the tray before placing Salmon***
  9. After 15 minutes, remove tray from oven and serve!
